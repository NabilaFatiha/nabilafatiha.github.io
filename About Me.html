Ultimate bucket list

When talking about dream job, yes I did have a few in mind. It is great to be ambitious and thinking the opportunity you'll meet in the future. As a reminder, wishing and hoping not enough in acting as a medium to achieve what you have been waiting for. Every dream should align with prayers and efforts. It is essential to constantly remind ourselves to be a hand to those in needs. Especially, the world currently battling with a tragic moment due to the pandemic. Some of them lost their loved ones or struggling to survive. We might wish our day could be better, but believe me there are few out there wishing to have your life. No matter what, be grateful and thankful to what we have. Talking about bucket list, it's a thing involved listing your most favourable wish. I did have one, which included going on a road trip at a foreign country. My first choice definitely Morocco, Iceland and New Zealand. People opinion about road trip are differ, since some of them thought it's a joyous activity and others think oppositely. Personally, to me, the journey undoubtedly must be tiring since you were constantly on the road. Just thinking all the wonderful moments awaiting during the upcoming time surely undescribable with words. Having a bucket list obliging in helping us to stay motivated to ensure we able to achieve a meaningful life and to not easily giving up. A documentary I've watched before which focuses on a cancer patient that have strong will to travel the world. At the same time, it gave him a piece of hope to continuously battling with the cancer. Most importantly, he did have a chance to fulfill the bucket list. Not to forget, individuals tend to misinterpret the concept of positivity. Staying positive does not meant to be happy always even during the toughest moment. Instead, it taught all of us to be patient and believe good day definitely come.
